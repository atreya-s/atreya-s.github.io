About me

I am a Senior at Case Western Reserve University and an undergraduate research Assistant at INVent Labs focusing on developing AI systems for medical imaging that are interpretable, reliable, and impactful for clinicians. Outside of work, I enjoy music, cooking and Urdu poetry. I am currently looking for full time positions, whether that be in data science/engineering or computer vision.

Research Interests

My research interests are deep learning and computer vision. I work on deep learning model architectures, training, optimization, and understanding. I also enjoy working on projects in mech interp and reinforcement learning, with an emphasis on vision models, but LLMs are very cool as well :)
